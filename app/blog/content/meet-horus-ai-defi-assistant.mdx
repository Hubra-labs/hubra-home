## DeFi has a UX problem

You&apos;ve felt it. The dashboards with 47 numbers you don&apos;t understand. The swap interface asking about slippage when you just want to buy SOL. The yield opportunity that looked great until you calculated the gas fees.

Most DeFi tools assume you already know what you&apos;re doing. They hand you power but demand expertise in return. The gap between "technically possible" and "actually usable" is where most people give up.

What if you could just tell your wallet what you want?

## How Horus works

Three steps:

1. You ask in plain English
2. Horus checks your balance, finds the best route, calculates costs
3. You review and sign

No hunting through menus. No remembering which DEX has better rates today. No manual slippage math.

Say "swap half my USDC to SOL" and Horus pulls your exact balance, routes through Jupiter for best execution, and shows the estimated output before you sign anything.

Say "swap all my USDC to SOL and then stake it" and Horus chains the transactions. One message, two operations.

## What Horus can do

### Portfolio intelligence

Ask about your holdings and get more than numbers.

Horus shows allocation breakdown, flags concentration risk and volatile token exposure, and tracks your recent activity. It tells you what those numbers mean for your situation, not just what they are.

### Trading

Horus handles token swaps through Jupiter with price impact and fee breakdown.

Set limit orders by saying "Buy 1 SOL at $50" and it creates an order that fills at that price. DCA is just as simple: "$500 into SOL over 5 daily orders" splits your entry automatically.

### Staking and yield

Stake SOL to raSOL via Sanctum with APY comparison. Instant unstake without waiting periods.

Browse earn vaults filtered by token, risk level, and APY. Deposit and withdraw through conversation.

### Perpetual trading

For leverage exposure: SOL, BTC, ETH futures up to 100x.

Long and short positions with clear risk info showing entry price, liquidation price, and unrealized PnL.

### Transfers and alerts

Send tokens by wallet address or saved contact. "Send 5 SOL to mom" works if mom is in your address book.

Set price alerts like "alert me when SOL drops below $100" and Horus monitors every 2 minutes.

All transactions on Hubra are gasless. Hubra covers the network fees.

Platform fees run 0.5-1% on swaps. Limit orders and DCA positions have a setup fee around 0.008 SOL.

No more "insufficient balance for gas" errors. No more keeping SOL aside just to pay for other transactions.

## Common questions

### Is Horus a wallet?

No. It works with your existing Hubra wallet. You sign every transaction. Horus builds them, you approve.

### What if something goes wrong?

Horus shows a detailed transaction card before you sign: tokens in/out, exchange rates, fees, price impact, estimated value. Nothing executes without your approval.

### Can Horus access my private keys?

No. Keys stay in your wallet. Horus builds transactions that you sign. It cannot move funds without your signature.

### What chains does Horus support?

Solana only right now. Deep integration with Jupiter for swaps, Sanctum for staking, and Hubra&apos;s earn vaults.

### How is Horus different from other AI assistants?

Most give advice. Horus executes. It connects to real DeFi protocols and submits real transactions.
